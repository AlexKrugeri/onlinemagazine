What is Bodyweight workout

Bodyweight workouts are made by exercises focused on strenght using only the body weight.
These exercises have the advantage that can be done everywhere, without any equipment and by anyone.

As we can see in the video, the ecercises are simple and easily executable by anyone.
This can be a reason to be more active and to keep ourselves more active.

Full body Workout
